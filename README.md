📒 AI Bookkeeping Assistant for SMEs
Overview

Our project is an AI-powered bookkeeping system that helps Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) manage their finances more efficiently.

Many small business owners struggle with bookkeeping because they lack accounting knowledge, have limited time, or cannot afford professional accounting software. Our solution simplifies financial management by allowing users to record transactions using voice commands while automatically generating financial insights and tax reports.

Problem Statement

Small businesses often face several challenges in managing their finances:

Manual bookkeeping is time-consuming.
Business owners may have little or no accounting knowledge.
Financial reports are difficult to prepare.
Tax submission can be confusing and prone to errors.
Existing accounting software is often expensive and complicated.

These issues lead to inaccurate financial records and poor business decision-making.

Our Solution

Our AI-powered bookkeeping assistant makes financial management simple through four main features:

🎤 Voice-Based Transaction Recording

Users can record sales and expenses using natural voice commands instead of manually entering data.

Example:

"Received RM250 from customer Ali."

The system automatically categorizes and stores the transaction.

📊 AI Financial Analysis

The AI analyzes recorded transactions and automatically generates insights, including:

Income summary
Expense breakdown
Cash flow overview
Spending patterns
Business improvement recommendations
🧾 Tax Report Generation

The system generates organized financial reports that can be used to prepare tax submissions quickly and accurately.

🌍 Accessible for Everyone

Designed with simplicity in mind:

Beginner-friendly interface
No accounting background required
Easy navigation
Suitable for SMEs and self-employed entrepreneurs
